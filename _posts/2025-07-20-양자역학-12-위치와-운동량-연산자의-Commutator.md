### 왜 $$[x,\hat{p}] = i\hbar$$인가?

$$
(x \hat{p} - \hat{p} x)f(x) = x\hat{p} f(x) - \hat{p}xf(x)
$$


$$
= x \left( -i\hbar \frac{\partial f(x)}{\partial x} \right) + i\hbar f(x) - x\left( -i\hbar \frac{\partial f(x)}{\partial x} \right)
$$


$$
= i\hbar f(x)
$$


$$
\therefore ~ x\hat{p} - \hat{p}x = i\hbar
$$
